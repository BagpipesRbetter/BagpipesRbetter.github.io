About Me

I’m Evan Barclay, an 18-year-old with a strong passion for technology, learning, and problem-solving. I bring a well-rounded technical background, with hands-on experience across Mac OS, Windows, and Linux systems, as well as proficiency in Python and Google App Script. Whether I’m diagnosing Chromebook issues, scripting tools to streamline education workflows, or troubleshooting home-built PCs, I approach every challenge with curiosity and a drive to improve.

My technical journey has been shaped by both academic and real-world experiences. As a tech intern at American Fork High School, I worked closely with the IT director and a technology teacher, gaining exposure to both hardware repair and software development. I also spent over a year working at Subway, where I developed strong communication skills, teamwork, and the ability to stay focused under pressure. Outside of work and school, I’m an avid gamer and tinkerer who loves digging into the details of how systems work. I’m currently preparing to attend Utah Valley University in Fall 2025, and I’m excited to keep building on my technical foundation in a collaborative, forward-thinking environment.
